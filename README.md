Task 1: AI Copy Generator for Graceful Glamour

AI Website Copy Generator for Local business 


## Business Profile

Business Name: Graceful Glamour Beauty Salon

Location: Tanjore, Tamil Nadu

Goal:

To become the go-to beauty destination for women in Tanjore by offering modern, hygienic, and confidence-boosting beauty services.

Target Audience:

Women, college students, and brides in Tanjore who are looking for stylish, affordable, and reliable beauty services.

## 2. Structured Prompt Design

Master Prompt

> Imagine yourself as a premium beauty salon copywriter for Graceful Glamour Beauty Salon located in Tanjore.


> 1.Create homepage content:
> - Write a bold and emotional headline focused on confidence and beauty
> - Write a sub-headline highlighting transformation and benefits
> - Write an engaging "About Us" section using storytelling style
> - Highlight hygiene, modern techniques, and personalized service


> 2.Create service page content:
> - Group services into categories (hair, skincare, bridal, nails)
> - Write benefit-driven descriptions for each service
> - Add a "Why Choose Us" section with strong points

> TA (Call-to-Action) sections:
> - Write an urgent CTA for quick bookings
> - Write a friendly CTA for regular customers
> - Write a professional CTA for bridal or premium services

> Tone Instructions:
> - Keep the tone modern, friendly, and confident
> - Avoid generic or robotic language
> - Make the content simple, clear, and persuasive
